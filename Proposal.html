All About the Bruins

Website Content

My website will be all about the hockey team the Boston Bruins. The first page will be the current roster and stats from 2021-2022. The second page will be links to videos of their greatest moments. The third page will be the greatest players in team hoistory along with their personal accolades and career statistics. The 4th page can include recent news for the team. The 5th page can include the most recent results and their upcoming schedule for the rest of the current season.

Website Objective

The objective of my website would be for people to learn about the Boston Bruins. It will also be a site where I will have links to other hockey websites that will have more information on the Bruins and the NHL.

Target Audience

My target audience would be NHL fans or fans of Boston sports who want to learn more about the Bruins as the NHL is the least popular of the four major sports in the US.
